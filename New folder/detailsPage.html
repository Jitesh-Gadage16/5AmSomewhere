Rules and Regulations

Stay with Love and Safety!

  • It is not allowed to include more people in the rental property than stated in the booking confirmation. Extra charges will apply at ₹ 5,000 per person per night, if at all it is within the hosting capacity of the villa.
  • Use safety lockers for valuables. The management and staff are unable to assume responsibility in case of any misplaced items.
  • If any keys are lost, the lock cylinders will be replaced by the locksmith for safety reasons and the same will be billed as actuals.
  • All staff must have at least 10 hours per 24 hours rest time. From 1 am to 7 am, all staff must retire to their rooms. 

Enjoy Amazing Food!

  • Our Chef is at your service! Ingredients & groceries will be charged as per actuals and bills will be provided to you based on your demand or kitchen requirements for your preferred choice of food. Please refer to the list of what our chef is best at. We provide one grocery run free of charge, per day.
  • Restaurants in Goa are happy to deliver food for you or to host you. Please call / WhatsApp our concierge team for reservations.

Stay with Responsibility!

  • Windows and doors should be closed unless needed to open (mosquitoes!). All lights and electronic devices should be turned off when not in use. It is a great habit of most guests which we appreciate!
  • We are sure you understand any damages are deductible from your security deposits.
  • Smoking inside the villa is strictly not allowed, however you can smoke in the balconies and the outside areas.
  • Got a pet? Please always have one guest to attend to your pet. (Prior approval is required, please check with us if you plan to get one)
  • On an occasion where any pollutants (we once had firecrackers make a mess!) if food or drinks that have fallen into the pool cleaning charge of ₹ 25,000 rupees is chargeable as it involves professional help, chemicals and water tankers.
  • Please don't move any indoor furniture outside to avoid any damages to your account.
  • Please don't put any decoration items that will tamper anything on the property.
  • Flammables and explosive materials and objects with unpleasant smell into the villa premises are prohibited. 
  • We strictly follow a no drugs policy along with any illegal/objectionable activities that are a reason of concern.
  • This villa does not permit any sort of parties/events, however we can help you organise sit-down dinners or barbecues with on-call chefs and a guest count not exceeding more than 4 people with prior permission.
  • Music has to be taken inside the villa between 10:00 pm - 08:00 am. It is legally not permitted and neighbours have a right to their peaceful rest while you enjoy your vacation. Our official permission to run a rental villa obliges us to follow these rules. Your cooperation and understanding is appreciated.
  • We have an open pool and no lifeguard on duty, the owners/management company cannot be held responsible for any mishaps, especially relating to children.
  • Use the furniture only after you've dried yourself up completely after using the pool.
  • We encourage our guests to not use and discard the towels, water conservation is our top priority.
